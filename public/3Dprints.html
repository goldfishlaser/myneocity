USB-A magnetic disconnect

Originally created to provide an open source decentralized manufactured kill cord for the Buskill project. I'm no longer directly associated with BusKill.in. That repo is available here.

My work on this project was published in Hill, Ash (May 02, 2023). "3D Print Your Own USB BusKill Module to Protect Data". Tom's Hardware and Halfacree, Gareth (May 08, 2024). "Melanie Allen Wants to Bring Dead-Man Switches to All with a 3D-Printable BusKill Adapter". hackster.io.

I present this project at DEF CON 32 in August 2024. I blog about it here: DEF CON 32 Announcement

DIY Prusa Mendel

I built a Prusa Mendel from an old Prusa and new parts. I managed to get a couple funky prints out of it, before it broke. In that time, I fried many stepper motors and other silly things. It's completely dissembled now, but I hope to one day reassemble it and make it into a pen plotter.
